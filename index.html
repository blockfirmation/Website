Our Solution: N-Trail

N-Trail is our flagship platform — a blockchain-agnostic Web3 ecosystem designed to bring digital trust, traceability, and resale functionality to physical products. We combine blockchain technology, NFTs and a user-friendly marketplace to deliver a full product lifecycle solution.

Phygital Product Passport (NFT-Backed)

We issue a unique non-fungible token (NFT) — a digital twin — for each physical product. This “phygital” NFT acts as a product passport, giving each item a permanent, verifiable record on the blockchain. It enables:

  • Authenticity verification for buyers
  • Proof of ownership and traceability
  • Secure resale or transfer of ownership
  • Warranty, service history and lifecycle tracking

N-Trail Marketplace & Business Portal

A marketplace and portal tailored for both end-customers and businesses — where brands and retailers can:

  • Mint phygital NFTs for their products
  • Publish and manage product listings
  • Track product ownership and resale activity
  • Engage customers post-purchase (e.g. warranty, loyalty, rewards)
  • Gain insights on customer demographics and product lifecycle analytics

Mobile App for Customers

Customers can use our mobile app to register, view, and manage their phygital NFTs — enabling them to verify authenticity, transfer items, resell or claim warranties and services easily.
