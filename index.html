NTrail - New Digital Trail of Real products

1. Counterfeit products are flooding the market, causing brands to lose revenue and customer trust. 2. Customers lack a reliable and transparent way to verify product authenticity and ownership. 3. Brands find it challenging to engage with customers and deliver product-based offers post purchase (e.g. warranties, service etc.) We leverage blockchain technology to create digital tokens and product passports for real-world products, ensuring verified proof of ownership for rightful owners.

Our Solution

NTRAIL powered by Blockchain & Web3 platform

Our first solution, NTrail will create a reliable online and Web3.0 platform for retail businesses, and its customers to sell or re-sell original premium products – authenticating genuine products that stand out from counterfeit products.

NTrail marketplace will enable access to all Phygital NFT’s (digital twin of a product) and our unique solution will offer businesses a platform to create Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) for their physical products and publish them on NTrail marketplace.

The marketplace can be publicly accessed and used as a platform to validate NTrail Phygital products and confirm authenticity. Our platform is mainly made of:

  1. NTRAIL Marketplace – A new NFT marketplace for Phygital products
  2. NTRAIL Mobile app – Unique mobile application to register Phygital NFTs, view your collection, transfer NFTs and access our marketplace
  3. NTRAIL Business Portal - New easy to use online website for creators and manufacturers to publish their Phygital NFTs on Web3 platform

Retail businesses and companies who are onboarded on to our platform will benefit from knowing their customer base and demography throughout the product lifecycle. Our solution is blockchain agnostic and predominantly applicable for premium products – however there are number of use cases we are currently exploring.
